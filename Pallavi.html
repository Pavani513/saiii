SAI PALLAVI

Sai Pallavi Senthamarai Kannan is an indian actress who primarily works in telugu,tamil and Malayalam films.She was featured by Forbes Magazine as one of india's 30 under 30 in 2020.Pallavi has established herself as a leading South Indian actress.
